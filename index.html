Bathroom Drain Stopper Repair

There was something clearly wrong with the drain plug itself. The lever that moved the plug up and down was stuck and would not budge up or down. I removed the faceplate, used a long screwdriver and leverage to slowly work the plug loose. The whole aparateus came out caked in rust and calcium deposits and it needed to be cleaned. I used the same polishing compound that was used on the Moscow Mule copper mugs and it worked like a charm. Returning the plug, it was much more free to move and the tub drained faster!
Mount the TV

The mount required finding the studs which were 16" apart from one another. I knew there was electric wires in the wall so I had to be careful with drilling through the drywall. Using the mounting template and a stud finder, I marked off the correct location for holes. After mounting the TV, I ran a coaxial cable through the wall to the attic where I connected a digital over-the-air antenna.
Paint and Polyurethene the IKEA kitchen table and chairs

The table needed to be sanded down, painted white, then coated with a protective layer of Polyurethene. A handheld power sander was used for most of the table. Once prepared, the painting process took 2 days to paint 3 coats of white and 2 coats of Polyurethene using an electric powered paint sprayer.
